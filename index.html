Classic Pancit Canton Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kilo of pork chopped
  • 1 carrots
  • 1 small cabbage
  • 2 pcs canton 1 kilo
  • 1 pack of oyster sauce
  • 1 chayote squash
  • 1 onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 pack of kickiam
  • 1 pack of chicken ball
  • soy sauce
  • pepper
  • msg

Instructions

  1. Heat the pork fat in a frying pan.
  2. Add the pork meat and cook until tender.
  3. Follow with the kikiam and chicken balls and cook thoroughly.
  4. Add garlic and onions, and let them turn golden brown.
  5. Add the vegetables, cook until tender.
  6. Season with a bit of soy sauce and oyster sauce, pepper and msg then set aside.
  7. In the same pan, pour water, a bit of soy sauce.
  8. Once the water boils, add the noodles and let them soften. And then put the remaining oyster sauce and msg.
  9. When cooked, add the vegetables and mix everything together.
  10. Serve while still hot.
